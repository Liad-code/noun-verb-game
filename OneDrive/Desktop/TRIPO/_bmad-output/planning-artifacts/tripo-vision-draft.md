## Executive Summary

Tripo is an AI-powered conversational web application designed to eliminate the "toggle tax" and decision fatigue inherent in modern travel planning. By providing a single chat interface where users can discuss their travel needs, preferences, and constraints, Tripo acts as a personalized travel agent that never forgets context. It aggregates live data across multiple providers (flights, hotels, activities) and presents unified, context-aware suggestions, replacing the fragmented experience of managing 5-10 different platform tabs. The MVP focuses on delivering a robust conversational interface integrated with flight and hotel searches across at least two major providers, targeting solo travelers, couples, and families.

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Travelers are forced to manually coordinate trip planning across multiple disconnected platforms (Kayak, Booking.com, Airbnb, etc.). This fragmented process requires repetitive data entry (dates, destinations, constraints) and forces users to mentally reconcile disjointed search results. There is no single place that remembers and applies personal context (e.g., "toddler," "window seat," "$2000 budget") across the entire travel booking ecosystem.

### Problem Impact

This fragmentation leads to significant friction:
- **Context Loss:** Users must re-enter the same information and preferences every time they switch platforms.
- **Decision Fatigue:** Travelers are overwhelmed by unfiltered options and struggle to compare "apples to oranges" accurately.
- **Suboptimal Bookings:** The cognitive load of manual comparison often results in users booking on one platform only to discover a better or cheaper option existed elsewhere.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

- **Google Travel:** Aggregates data well but lacks a conversational layer and personalized memory for nuanced preferences.
- **General AI (ChatGPT/Gemini):** Offers conversational planning and generic suggestions but cannot execute live price searches or facilitate actual bookings.
- **Traditional Travel Agents:** Provide personalized service but are expensive, slow, and lack 24/7 availability.
- **Single-Vertical Tools (Hopper/Roam):** Have entirely too narrow of a focus (e.g., only flights or price prediction) and don't solve the full-trip coordination problem.

### Proposed Solution

Tripo bridges these gaps by offering a **unified, conversational AI travel companion** with live inventory access. Users interact naturally with the AI, which holds full trip context. Tripo searches simultaneously across multiple APIs (Kayak, Kiwi, Skyscanner, Booking.com) and presents cohesive, tailored results—all without the user ever leaving the chat interface.

### Key Differentiators

- **Persistent Context:** The AI remembers nuanced preferences (dietary needs, seating, travel companions) and applies them globally to all searches.
- **Unified Interface:** Replaces the multi-tab browser nightmare with a single, conversational checkout flow.
- **Actionable AI:** Moves beyond generic itinerary generation by directly integrating with live booking APIs for real-world execution.
