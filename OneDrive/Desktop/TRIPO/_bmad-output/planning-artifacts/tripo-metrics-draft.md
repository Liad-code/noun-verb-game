## Success Metrics

To ensure Tripo is creating real value for users and achieving business objectives, we will track the following success criteria:

### User Success Metrics
- **Actionable Outcomes:** Session completes with at least 1 affiliate link clicked.
- **Speed to Value:** Time from the first conversational message to an affiliate link click is under 10 minutes.
- **High Intent Signals:** User actively saves a trip to their account.
- **Retention:** User returns to plan a second trip within 90 days.

### Business Objectives
**3 Months Post-Launch:**
- Acquire 500 registered users.
- Achieve 100+ affiliate link clicks per month.
- Successfully deploy 2 live API provider integrations.

**12 Months Post-Launch:**
- Scale to 10,000 registered users.
- Generate €5,000/month in affiliate revenue.
- Expand to 4+ live provider integrations.
- Maintain a consistent 20% month-over-month user growth rate.

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Affiliate CTR:** 40%+ of completed chat sessions result in at least 1 affiliate link click.
- **Return Rate:** 30%+ of users return to plan a second trip within 90 days.
- **Chat Completion Rate:** 70%+ of started conversations successfully reach the curated results stage.
- **Average Session Value:** Track the estimated booking value per affiliate click for accurate revenue forecasting.
- **Provider Coverage:** Ensure a baseline of at least 2 flight providers and 1 hotel provider live at the MVP launch.
